Prisoner's Dilemma: The Game
Rules
1. Each turn, choose to either take the money or pass.
2. If only one player takes the money, it is added to their score and the pot resets.
3. If both players pass, the money doubles.
4. If both players try to take the money, both players lose all their money.
5. You cannot take the money if you took money last turn.
